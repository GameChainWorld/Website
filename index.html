ABSTRACT

The goal of GAMECHAIN is to build a Blockchain game content public community which is of the people, by the people, for the people. Users can make games, paly games, exchange games, establish digital property rights and make transactions. The community uses smart contracts, decentralized consensus approach UGC (User Generate Content) to make contribution pricing and equity returns, so that content producers, content filtering reviewers, community promoters, and players can all get reasonable incentives and promote community's long-term sound development and growth. Currently, GAMECHAIN has its own game production tool "BCreator.io"(An internal test version has been completed).

GAMECHAIN的目标是建设一个民有、民治、民享的区块链游戏内容公共社区，用户可以做游戏、玩游戏、交流游戏、进行数字财产确权及交易。社区通过智能合约去中心化共识方式UGC（User Generate Content），进行贡献定价和权益回报，使内容生产者、内容筛择评论者、社区推广者、玩家都能得到合理的激励，促使社区长期良性发展及壮大。目前GAMECHAIN已拥有自己的游戏制作工具“BCreator.io”（已完成内部测试版本）。
